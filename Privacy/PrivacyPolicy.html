Privacy Policy for My TikTok Data Exporter

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of My TikTok Data Exporter regarding the collection, use, and handling of personal information. Our goal is to be a trustworthy steward of any personal information involved in the operation of this application.

The collection and use of personal information are essential for the functionality of My TikTok Data Exporter. While performing this critical work, we are committed to upholding privacy protections. This policy is based on widely recognized fair information practice principles and aims to reflect our aspirational values in handling personal information.

Please note that this Privacy Policy is intended to provide transparency about our data practices and does not create any contractual or other legal obligation on our part, nor any contractual or other legal right or expectation for any individual person.

What is Personal Information?

Personal information is any data that relates to an identifiable individual person, including their character traits, history, and activities. This not only includes direct identifiers (like a name) but also indirect information (such as purchasing history, Internet activity, or, in some cases, information about personal activities). For this application, personal information primarily refers to your own TikTok user data that can identify you or relate to your account activities.

Information We Collect

The My TikTok Data Exporter script is designed to collect specific information directly from your TikTok account. This information is limited to:

  • Your own TikTok user data: This includes your profile information (e.g., username, bio, follower count) and metadata related to your videos (e.g., video titles, descriptions, view counts, likes, comments).

Important: This application is designed for personal use to export *your own* data. It does not collect data from other TikTok users.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected by My TikTok Data Exporter is used solely for the following purpose:

  • To display your TikTok video performance and account metrics in a Google Sheet for your personal analysis and record-keeping.

The data is processed locally within the script's execution environment (e.g., Google Apps Script) and then outputted directly to a Google Sheet that you control.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your data with any third parties. All data processing occurs within the confines of your script's execution environment and your Google Sheet, which you control.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

As a purely server-side script (like Google Apps Script), My TikTok Data Exporter does not use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities or interactions with websites.

Integration with TikTok

This application integrates directly with the TikTok platform to retrieve your data. Your use of TikTok's services is subject to their own policies. We encourage you to review:

  • TikTok's Privacy Policy: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • TikTok's Terms of Service: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/terms-of-service
